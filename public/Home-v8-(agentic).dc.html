Questions

Good to know.

What does "agentic" actually mean here?
Each discipline — search, ads, web, conversion — has a dedicated agent that monitors its channels continuously and prepares the next best move. A senior strategist validates everything, and nothing ships until you approve. Agents propose; humans dispose.
Do the agents post or spend without me?
No. Every meaningful change waits for your approval. Once you trust a pattern you can let routine moves auto-approve, but you set that line — and you can move it back anytime.
Why include ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini?
Buyers increasingly ask AI assistants for recommendations before they ever hit Google. The SEO & Answer agent works to get your business cited in those answers, not just ranked in classic search.
Do I own my accounts and data?
Always. Your website, content, ad accounts, and data are yours outright. We operate them on your behalf — we never hold them hostage.
Am I locked into a contract?
No. Plans are month-to-month. Your free audit returns a flat monthly number and the revenue we expect to find, so you decide with real figures.
