Before you start the engine

Fair questions.

"Autopilot" sounds like the AI runs wild. Does it?
No. The engine finds and prepares; a human strategist validates; and nothing ships until you approve. Autopilot means the work runs continuously — not that decisions happen without you.
Can I let routine things ship automatically?
Yes. Once you trust a pattern — small ad shifts, content updates — you can set it to auto-approve and reserve your taps for the bigger calls. You set the line.
Do I need to learn the console?
If you can read a text message, you can run it. Each opportunity is one line, one estimate, one tap. There's nothing to configure and no dashboard to babysit.
What does it cost, and am I locked in?
Month-to-month, no long contracts, and you own your site, content, ad accounts, and data. Your free audit returns a flat monthly number and the revenue we expect to find.
