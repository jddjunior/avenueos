# Branch Avenue — Voice lexicon per vertical (F5)

One calm register, four vocabularies. The same claim is said in the buyer's
own units — never in ours. Rule of thumb: if the sentence could appear in any
SaaS deck, rewrite it with a unit from the buyer's column.

## Dealership principals / GMs (Avenue OS)
- **Their units:** rooftops, stores, gross, PVR, floor traffic, the desk, OEM co-op.
- **Never say:** users, conversion events, engagement.
- **Register:** peer operator. They've been burned by vendor tools; lead with numbers by store.
- *"Every rooftop on one pane of glass — leads, gross, and inventory by store, by month."*

## HVAC & home-services owners (AtHome)
- **Their units:** booked jobs, trucks, crews, callbacks, service area, the season.
- **Never say:** pipeline optimization, digital transformation.
- **Register:** straight-talking neighbor. They distrust software salesmen; respect their time.
- *"Quotes go out while your crews are still on the job. No evening paperwork."*

## Franchise / multi-location operators
- **Their units:** locations, compliance, rollout waves, brand standards, corporate vs. field.
- **Never say:** disruption, reinvention. They buy control and consistency, not novelty.
- **Register:** systems person. Show hierarchy: HQ sees everything, each location sees its own.
- *"Corporate sees the whole system. Each location only sees their own."*

## Agency & Shopify clients
- **Their units:** clients, retainers, churn, white-label, the monthly report.
- **Never say:** end-users. They are reselling us — arm them to look good to *their* clients.
- **Register:** capable partner backstage. Their brand forward, ours invisible.
- *"White-label reports your clients actually forward to their boss."*

## Agent & strategist microcopy (F7·C doctrine)
- Agent messages state **what was handled**, past tense, no drama:
  *"Three agents watched your funnel overnight. Two things were handled. Nothing needs you before 10am."*
- Strategist notes explain **why in one sentence**, signed with a face:
  *"Your Saturday traffic converts twice as well as weekdays — this just moves budget to match. Easy yes."*
- Empty states **reassure, never apologize**:
  *"All clear. Everything's running the way it should — enjoy the afternoon."*
