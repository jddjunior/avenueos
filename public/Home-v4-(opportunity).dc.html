FAQ

Questions owners ask first.

What is an opportunity assessment?

A free analysis where our AI scans your website, search presence, ads, and customer journey to surface the highest-value revenue opportunities currently available — ranked by estimated value and ready for your review. No obligation.

Do humans review what the AI finds?

Yes. Every opportunity is reviewed and validated by a senior strategist before it reaches you, and nothing is acted on without your approval. Agents propose; humans dispose.

How are the dollar estimates calculated?

Estimates are modeled from your real traffic, conversion rates, and average customer value — then shown with a confidence score so you know how solid each projection is before you commit.

Am I locked into a contract?

No. Plans are month-to-month, and you own your website, content, and data. If you ever leave, you walk away with everything.
